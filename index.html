about us

5 and a half years of experience

Founded five and a half years ago, Al-Miraaj Secondary School has been dedicated to fostering academic excellence and personal growth. Our experienced faculty and comprehensive curriculum ensure that each student reaches their full potential. Over the years, we have grown into a reputable institution known for our commitment to education and student development.

At Al-Miraaj Secondary School, we offer a wide range of academic and extracurricular programs designed to engage and inspire our students. From STEM courses to arts and athletics, there is something for everyone.

Computer studies

The Computer Studies curriculum provides students with a strong foundation in computer principles and digital literacy. Through practical projects and theoretical learning, students gain the skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving field of technology.

Our Mathematics program focuses on developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Our Business program introduces students to the essential concepts of economics, finance, management, and entrepreneurship.
